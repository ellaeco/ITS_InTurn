# Housing, Transit, and Ann Arbor
## Housing
If you are looking for summer housing in Ann Arbor, there are a few places to look.
* [UMICH Housing, Rooms, Apartments, Sublets | Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/groups/223351171575348/): There is no shortage of students moving back home for the summer, meaning there is also an influx of students posting on Facebook to sublet their housing, often at a discounted rate.
* [Intern Housing for Summer Programs](https://conferences.umich.edu/summer/intern-housing/): U-M also offers summer housing for interns affiliated with a University of Michigan department or entity. Dorms are on-campus and include laundry, Wi-Fi, and maintenance service, with no utility fees.
### Tips for finding housing
* **Start looking early.** It is a good idea to start your housing search as soon as possible after accepting the internship to secure the housing option that works best for you.
* **Know where you will be working.** If possible, ask your supervisor prior to selecting housing about where you will primarily be working, or if you will be mostly remote.
* **Weigh out your expenses.** Consider additional expenses that may incur, including commuting, parking, and utilities, then decide what option works best for your budget.
## Transit
With your yellow MCard, you receive unlimited free access to TheRide buses, which services the greater Ann Arbor-Ypsilanti area. Simply swipe your MCard once boarding the bus and you’re good to go.
### Resources for navigating transit
* TheRide: Track your bus, plan your trip, view routes, and stay up to date on alerts.
* Rider Guide: Browse additional resources on transit etiquette and trip planning.
