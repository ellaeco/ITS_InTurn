# ITS Internship Program Overview
Welcome to the ITS Internship Program! This 14-week summer experience provides students with hands-on professional experience, networking opportunities, mentorship, and collaborative project work.

## Program at a Glance
* Duration: 14 weeks (May–August)
* Typical Hours: Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM
* Work Format: Hybrid or fully in-person, depending on your department and supervisor
* Professional Development: Required in-person attendance at least twice per week
* Summer Classes: Must not conflict with professional development sessions

## What to Expect
### **Department Experience**
Interns spend most of their time working within their assigned department, contributing to projects that support university operations and initiatives.
### **Professional Development**
Professional development sessions are held twice per week for approximately two hours each session
### **Mentorship Program**
Each intern is paired with a mentor outside of their department. Mentors provide career guidance, networking opportunities, resume support, and professional development advice. 
### **Orientation**
The internship begins with a full-day Orientation where interns meet supervisors, learn about cohort projects, receive professional headshots, and begin networking. 
### **Cohort Project**
Project sponsors present opportunities during Orientation. Interns work with fellow interns throughout the summer and should expect to spend 4–6 hours per week on project work. 
### **Showcase Presentation**
At the end of the summer, interns present their accomplishments, project outcomes, and lessons learned to university faculty and staff. 

## Summer Goals
* Complete department projects
* Complete cohort projects
* Present at the Internship Showcase
* Build professional and technical skills
* Expand your network
