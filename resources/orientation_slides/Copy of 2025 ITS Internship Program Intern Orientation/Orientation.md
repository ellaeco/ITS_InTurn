# Orientation Day for New Interns
Orientation is located at the Arbor Lakes building: 4251 Plymouth Rd, Ann Arbor, MI, 48105 in Building 3, the Dome Room
You can park in the large lot in front of Buildng 3, and parking is free.
The dress code for Orientation Day is business professional. Dress code for professional development is business casual, so you need to dress well for both, but moreso for orientation
## Orientation Day Schedule 
* 8:00-8:30 AM: Check In/Coffee/Donuts/Networking
* 8:30-10:00 AM: Kickoff/Important Information/Program Components
* 9:50-10:05 AM: Break
* 10:05-10:50 AM: Mentorship Presentation
* 10:50-11:00 AM: Introdruce What's Next & Break
* 11:00-2:00 PM: "Scavenger Hunt" which includes a lunch with your supervisor, headshots, laptop distribution and set up, Cohort Project Poster Session, Self-Study Objectives, Ice cream truck, and collect 'swag'
* 2:00-EOD: First pitches and presentations
