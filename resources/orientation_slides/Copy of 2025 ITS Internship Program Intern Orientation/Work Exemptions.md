# Work Exemptions
## Holidays 
* Interns will not work on holidays and are not eligible for compensation for them. Most interns work four ten-hour days within the same week to make up holiday time.
## Vacations 
* Vacation time will be unpaid and requires prior approval from the intern's supervisor and the internship team lead. Inters are expecred to limit vacation time to seven days or fewer.
## Working outside the state/country
* Interns cannot work outside the state of Michigan or outside the country at any time, even remotely. If an intern must leave the state or country, they CANNOT work. We will communicate this with inters, but need the support of supervisors to reinforce it.
## Professional Development time is Program time
* Interns cannot make up missed prefessional development as it is program time, not departmental time.
## Summer classes 
* If you are taking a summer class that causes you to miss departmental time, being allowed to make up that time is at your supervisor's discretion. Missed departmental time must be made up within the same week it was missed. Even though the university operates on a bi-weekly pay period, interns cannot flex or make up time across Week 1 and Week 2 of that period. Make-up time must occur in the same calendar week as the absence
## ESTA (Expanded Sick Time Accrual) Policy for U-M Student Employees 
### Student employees at the University of Michigan, including interns, accrue paid sick time under the ESTA policy. ESTA sick time can be used for: 
* The intern's own illness, injury, or medical condition
* Medical or dental appointments for the intern
* Caring for an immediate family member (such as a child, spouse, or parent) due to illness, injury, or medical appointment
* Absence due to a public health emergency (e.g., quarantine or isolation ordered by a health official)
* Reason related to domestic violence, sexual assault, or stalking- including seeking medical care, counseling, relocation, or legal assistance for the intern or an immediate family member
### Time Reporting: ESTA time is reported using the SCE time reporting code. There are eligibility requirements that should be verified before an intern uses ESTA time. If you have any questions, need clarification, or would like to confrim eligibility, please reach out to James Markus.
