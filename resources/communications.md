# 2025 ITS Internship Program: Communications Guide

## Overview
Effective communication is key to a successful internship at ITS. This guide outlines the primary channels of communication, the specific roles of the planning team, and who you should engage for specific types of support.

**General Rule:** If you are unsure who to ask, start with the **Program Coordinator (Claire Tucker)** or the **Intern Liaison (Kieran Haas)**.

---

## 📞 Primary Point of Contact
For most questions, logistics, or concerns regarding your internship experience, your first stop is the Community Programs Outreach Coordinator.

### **Claire Tucker**
* **Role:** Oversees planning, programming, logistics, and execution.
* **When to engage:** * General questions about the program schedule.
    * Logistical issues.
    * Concerns about your experience (issues/challenges).
* **Note:** *"If I can’t answer it, I will help you find someone who can!"*
* **Contact:** Slack or `clairetu@umich.edu`
* **Office:** Arbor Lakes

> **Feedback Protocol:** The planning team notes that general concerns or "negative" feedback should be directed to **Claire Tucker**, while **Jared Walfish** jokingly requests to be contacted "with only positive things."
