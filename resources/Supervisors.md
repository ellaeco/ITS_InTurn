#Information for Potential and Current Supervisors

##Required Supervisory Duties
###The supervisor commits to the following responsibilities:
Hold regular meetings with interns.
Approve bi-weekly timesheets promptly. Interns will not be paid unless their timesheet is approved.
Conduct mid-point and final performance evaluations.
Provide guidance and feedback.
Ensure clear communication about the program's components.
Keep a constant line of communication open.
Communicate expectations clearly, including developing a work plan (objectives & responsibilities) and performance management guidelines.
Attend at least one supervisor check-in per month.
Attend an in-person intern orientation lunch at the beginning of May.
Ensure your intern’s laptop is returned to ITS Support Services at the end of their appointment (including if they’re extended past the summer).

##Coach/Daily Direction Expectations
###If the supervisor also serves as the intern's coach, the following apply:
Meet with interns frequently during the week and provide day-to-day direction.
Maintain the assignment sheet and deliverables list. This list of project assignments, deliverables, and the onboarding checklist must be submitted to the Internship Lead Team.
Serve as the point of contact for resources and training.
Serve as the first line for escalation of questions and issues.
