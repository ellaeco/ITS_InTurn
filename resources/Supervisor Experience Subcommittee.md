# Responsibilities for the ITS Supervisor Experience Subcommittee

## Supervisor Recruitment
Begin recruiting supervisors through advertising and word of mouth before applications for interns opens in early November.

## Leading up to the Internship Program
Keep communicating with supervisors and make sure that they are aware of the responsibilities of hosting an intern. Make sure that they are kept informed of the status of interviews and are looped in to the necessary conversations about the potential interns for their positions. Once interns sign their offers, be available for any questions from supervisors about the next steps in preparing for their interns.

## Throughout the Internship Program
Be available for any questions from supervisors or coaches throughout the program. Some common questions are about the process to approve timesheets, problems with taking time off, and questions about working out of state.
