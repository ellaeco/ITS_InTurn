# Weekly Cadence
**[Home](../README.md)** 
___

The plan is to release your weekly assignments on Saturday morning at 9:00am. You will have until Saturday at 8:00am to complete them. We will have a brief standup at 9:00am on most days. You will have a weekly team meeting with your Rails team on Thursday at 9:00am.

- Core hours are 8:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m. and 1:30 p.m. - 5:00 p.m. You are expected to be available and ready to work during this time. If you have extenuating circumstances, let your supervisor know as soon as possible.
- Reflections are due (committed to your GitHub repository) before 8:00 am Saturday.
- Next week's syllabus will be released on Saturday at 8:00am.
- You will have a weekly check-in with your mentor on Friday at 9:30am.
