# UM ITS Departments: Who They Are and What They Do

## Administration and Operations

Supports ITS Staff and university operations so ITS can function effectively.

**Sub Departments:**

* **Community Programs Outreach:** Manages student internships and staff engagement programs.
* **Contract Management and Purchasing:** Handles contracts, vendor relationships, and procurement.
* **Facilities:** Maintains and renovates ITS buildings and workspaces.
* **Finance:** Oversees budgeting, compensation planning, and forecasting.
* **Human Resources:** Manages hiring, employee relations, compensation, and HR consulting.
* **Marketing and Communications:** Handles communications, branding, marketing, and public affairs.
* **Organizational Development:** Focuses on staff development, training, and change management.

**Interns:**

* ITS Administration and Operations: 1 intern
* ITS Marketing and Communications: 3 interns

---

## Advanced Research Computing (ARC)

Provides high-performance computing and advanced technology support for research.

**Sub Departments:**

* **Data Science Consulting:** AI models, software development, and training.
* **Federal & External Awards Support:** Helps researchers with grant applications and support.
* **High Performance Computing (HPC):** Includes Great Lakes (open science computing), Armis2 (secure computing for protected data), and Lighthouse (researcher-owned hardware integrated into HPC).
* **Research Networking & Integration:** Connects researchers to national computing providers.
* **Research Mission Support:** Coordinates scalable IT services for research labs.
* **Secure Enclave Service:** Secure environment for highly regulated data (PHI, CUI, NIST 800-171).
* **Sensitive Data Support:** HIPAA/PHI-approved services beyond the enclave.

## Data Science Practice

Provides reporting, analytics, data integration, and business intelligence services.

**Sub Departments:**

* **Business Intelligence Tools:** Tableau, BusinessObjects, Denodo, and M-Reports.
* **BI & Data Analytics Training:** Teaches reporting and visualization best practices.
* **Data Architecture:** Designs data models and enterprise data structures.
* **Data Concierge Service:** Helps users access institutional data.
* **Data Integration & Delivery:** Maintains enterprise data warehouse and ETL systems.
* **API Directory:** Provides APIs for institutional data access.

**Interns:**

* ITS CIO Privacy and Faculty Affairs: 1 intern

## Enterprise Application Services (EAS)

Manages enterprise software systems that support university operations.

**Sub Departments:**

* **Application Infrastructure & Operations:** Supports enterprise system infrastructure and integrations.
* **Communications & Marketing Systems:** Manages imaging and digital asset systems.
* **Customer Relationship Management (Salesforce):** Centralized stakeholder relationship platform.
* **Development and Alumni Relationship Tool (DART):** Fundraising and alumni engagement system.
* **Financials & Physical Resources (FIN):** Financial and supply chain systems.
* **Human Capital Management (HCM):** HR systems and My LINC learning platform.
* **Mobile & Web Application Development:** Develops apps like Michigan App and VaxViewer.
* **Performance Support:** Training, communications, and documentation for systems.
* **Research Administration Systems (eResearch):** Research proposal, compliance, and award management.
* **Student Administration (SA):** Enrollment, curriculum, financial aid, and student systems.

**Interns:**

* ITS Enterprise Applications Services: 5 interns
* ITS eResearch: 1 intern

## Information Assurance (IA)

Responsible for cybersecurity and risk management across the university.

**Sub Departments:**

* **Compliance Support:** HIPAA, FISMA, CUI, and regulatory compliance.
* **Incident Response & Threat Intelligence:** Manages cyber incidents and threat sharing.
* **IT Security Operations:** Security consulting for university units.
* **Network Security:** Protects campus networking systems.
* **Risk Assessment:** Conducts security assessments and risk analysis.
* **University-wide Education:** Security awareness training.
* **Vulnerability Management:** Penetration testing and security scanning.

**Interns:**

* ITS Information Assurance: 4 interns

## Infrastructure

Builds and maintains campus networking and telecommunications systems.

**Sub Departments:**

* **Contact Centers:** Builds and supports call/contact center systems.
* **Data Centers:** Hosts compute and storage infrastructure.

* **Information Systems:** Billing and ordering systems.

* **Television & Streaming:** Live content and entertainment systems.
