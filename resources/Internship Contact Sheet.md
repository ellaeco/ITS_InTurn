# Contact Sheet

> Effective communication is key to a successful internship at ITS. This guide outlines the primary channels of communication, the specific roles, and who you should engage for specific types of support. Get to know who works for and runs ITS, and record their contact information and the ways they can help a possible intern.

---

## Key Contacts

| Name / Team | Role | Email | Purpose / When to Contact |
|---|---|---|---|
| ITS Internship Planning Team  | Program Coordination | its-internship-planning@umich.edu | Official internship questions, onboarding, scheduling, placement issues, approvals, and program-wide communication |
| Intern Peer Group | Intern Cohort | *(Given before internship starts)* | Ask other interns questions, share tips, collaborate, and peer support |
| Assigned Mentor | Internship Mentor | *(assigned per intern)* | Guidance on projects, technical direction, career advice, feedback, and professional development |
| Internship Supervisor | Direct Manager | *(assigned per intern)* | Day-to-day project supervision, performance feedback, task assignments, and escalation within the team |
| ITS Service Center | Technical Support | its-help@umich.edu | Password resets, account access, system issues, technical troubleshooting |
| ITS Information Assurance | Cybersecurity Support | security@umich.edu | Security-related issues, vulnerabilities, and compliance questions |
