### Reflections
[Weekly reflections](../../resources/reflections.md) are due by 8am Saturday. Please submit your reflection via commit to a branch on this repository and submit a pull request when you are ready to share it. [How to submit your work](../../resources/submitting_your_work_via_github.md)

This week, in addition to your ***W&ADS Weekly Whats:tm:***  ; use the prompt **'In the fall I...'** as a starting point for this week's reflection. What are you planning on doing in the fall? What coursework will you be pursuing (if any)? Are you planning on working? Where would you like to work? (**NOTE: the GSRA and GSI gigs are pretty nice if you are doing your graduate work here.) Do you want to launch your startup dungeons and dragons AI generated scenario generator? Do you want to take a break and travel? Do you want to take a break and do nothing? I could go on, especially with GitHub copilot :tm: offering suggestions...
