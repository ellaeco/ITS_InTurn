# Phase-0 Guide

**[Week-1](week-1/README.md)** | **[Week-2](week-2/README.md)** | **[Week-3](week-3/README.md)** 

**Welcome to Phase 0!** We hope you are excited to further your knowledge and begin acclimating to the ITS Internship Program. For each of the next three weeks we will provide resources to help you to orient to the program, access resources that will help you on your journey, and prepare you for success in the ITS Internship program.  The overall goal is to lay the foundation for having a successful internship program. Phase 0 is also is about building rapport and trust. It is about helping the new team members feel confident asking for help. 

During this phase the team members will: 
  - Introduce themselves to the internship community
  - [meet thier supervisor, mentor and fellow interns (virtually)](../resources/meet_the_team_exercise.md)
  - learn about our mission, values and expectations
  - learn about resources to support their summer (housing, transportation, technology)
  - learn how to communicate with the planning team and their sponsoring department.

In addition, be exposed to resources to help orient you to some core concepts that will help you working in information technology, and working in general.
